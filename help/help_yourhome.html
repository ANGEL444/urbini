Your Home - all your watches, alerts, tasks, subscriptions
 
Dashboard Your login name will appear at the top left of all screens. Click on your name for your own personal dashboard. Everything relating to you will be listed here. You can drill into your watches, issues and assignments.
     
Password Your password was set for you, but you may change it. From your dashboard click on your "screen name". This will show you your user details (note this is different from your contact details). Select edit from the File menu, change the password and save.
Contact details You can update any of your contact details from your dashboard using the edit menu.
Messages Any workflow items will be listed in your approved, pending rejected or returned folders.  
Inbox This is your email inbox. Any message relating to you will can be found here. You can use it to handle your daily workload.
Your resources This shows every resource type that you have either asked to be a part of or have been invited/assigned to. It also shows any reports you've scheduled or watches you've set up.
