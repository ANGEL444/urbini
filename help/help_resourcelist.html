Lists - viewing resource lists of any type
Summary A resource list shows the key details for any class of information. You will generally see 20 resources per page. At the top it shows how many in total you have listed and where in the list you are. At the top and bottom you can select the next page in the list. Also at the bottom you can jump to any page you like if the list has many pages.
Sorting Lists can be sorted in any order by clicking on the column icon or name. You will see an up or down arrow appear next to the icon. The first click on an unsorted column will sort in ascending order. Click it again to sort in descending order.
Links Click on the left hand icon of the resource to see the full details of the resource including any change history, comments, attachments & subscription details.
Hovering Most items shown will give you more information when you hover over them. Column icons will show you their name and dates will show you the full date and time where applicable. Text that has been shortened will end with a double chevron >>. Hovering over the text will show you it completely.
