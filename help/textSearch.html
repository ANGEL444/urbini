Quick searches (see more details)
Single words red You can enter single words as a search. It will only look for the exact word or set of characters. In this example it will only look for red, not redder or shred
All words red AND blue Use AND to find resources that contain all the words you're searching for. This example will only find things with both red and blue in.
Any words red blue Entering multpile words seperated by OR will find results with any of the words you entered. This will find anything with either red or blue. OR is the default search and isn't normally required.
Exact words "red blue" Entering multpile words in double quotes will find the exact text you entered. This will only find resources with "red blue" next to each other.
incomplete words red* Find words that start with your characters. This will find red & reduce
Includes Y/N +red -blue This allows you to find things that must or must not include a certain word. This example finds things that might contain the word red but does not include the word blue.
max words apart "words close"~10 Use this technique to find words that appear in the same sentence or close together in a document. This example would find this help page.
