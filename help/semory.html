I. Semory is a smart storage - it understands what it stores and can help you locate a piece of information by any bit of data you might have at hand . Once located, Semory helps you navigate, analyze and discover patterns in data. It gives a feeling of control similar to a GPS-in-a-car integrated with the map and actively guiding you through the streets.

II. Semory is also an active storage since it is watching all the information that is going in and out of it. It executes biz rules and alerts (email, online inbox, real-time alerts, etc.) its Users on changes and scheduled items.

III. As a User all you need to interact with the Semory is a Web Browser (and e-mailer).
