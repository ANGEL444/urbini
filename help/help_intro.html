Welcome to MRM, the collaborative platform
   
 

We currently use email, phone and fax for all the job planning, definition and revision information from customers. If you're a member of the a production planning email groups you will know how many pieces of information make up a job and how many revisions come through. Keeping track of the latest updates, making sure they have been actioned by someone and having all the information to hand has been a time consuming but critical task for everyone involved.

The intranet (Vantage) is mainly a read only monitoring, reporting and analysis web tool for us and the customers. We are introducing a new dimension to Vantage enabling better collaboration between print site operations and customers. MRM is a new web platform which will enable us to change the way we interact with customers & colleagues. (In case you're wondering, MRM stands for Multi-media Relationship Management. The buzz word in business at the moment is CRM which means Customer Relationship Management, but MRM goes way beyond this fixed boundary.)

The initial goals for MRM are as follows:
1 Standardise the print notices, instructions and acknowledgements we handle with customers
2 Standardise the special care notice procedure for customers and printers
3 Centralise issue management relating to a job whether they are publisher, production or print issues
4 Manage print test and trial procedures, reporting and operations

MRM allows production and the customers to track changes and see who changed what, and when. It allows management and the printers to have all the relevant information to hand instantly, the moment it's happened.

You will start to see some new icons on the Vantage Web Pages where you can drill into MRM Issues, Print Notices, Print Trials and Special Care Notices. The links will appear on the issue report, future scheduled jobs and the press running screen. These links are for read only access but the full system is available via a login.
